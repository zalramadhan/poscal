# INVENTORY AI

Goal:

Prevent stockouts.

Features:

- Stock Prediction
- Reorder Suggestions
- Slow Moving Products
- Dead Stock Detection

Inputs:

- Sales History
- Current Stock
- Lead Time

Outputs:

- Suggested Purchase Quantity
- Predicted Out Of Stock Date
